The 4090 offers 16,384 of the 18,432 cores and 24 GB GDDR6X graphics memory with a 384 bit memory bus and clocked at 21 Gbits. The card offers an excellent raytracing performance thanks to the 128 dedicated raytracing cores. For AI calculations (and DLSS 3 in games), the RTX 4090 has 512 Tensor cores.
